Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our platform and services

How do I register as a supplier on SA TradeHub?

To register as a supplier, click on the "Join Free" button at the top of the page and fill out the supplier registration form. You'll need to provide your business details, contact information, and products you wish to list. Our team will verify your information before activating your account.

What are the fees for using SA TradeHub?

Basic membership is free and allows you to create a company profile and list up to 10 products. We offer premium membership tiers with additional features such as enhanced visibility, product showcase, and advanced analytics. Transaction fees may apply for certain payment processing services.

How can I verify a supplier's credibility?

We offer several verification badges for suppliers including "Verified Business," "Gold Supplier," and "Assessed Supplier." Look for these badges on supplier profiles. You can also check their transaction history, customer reviews, and response rate to assess their reliability.

What payment methods are supported?

We support various payment methods including bank transfers, credit cards, and our secure Escrow service which holds payment until you confirm receipt of goods. We're also integrated with popular South African payment gateways for local transactions.

Do you offer logistics and shipping services?

Yes, we partner with leading logistics providers across South Africa and internationally. Through our platform, you can compare shipping rates, track shipments, and manage logistics for both domestic and international trade.
